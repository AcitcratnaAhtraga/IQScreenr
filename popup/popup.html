How It Works

Uses a research-based estimator with 4 dimensions (trained on graded samples):

  • Vocabulary Sophistication (35%): Age of Acquisition proxies via word length and syllable complexity
  • Lexical Diversity (25%): Type-Token Ratio (TTR) - measures word variety
  • Sentence Complexity (20%): Average words per sentence
  • Grammatical Precision (20%): Syntax complexity via punctuation and subordinate clauses

Fully client-side: All processing happens in your browser. No data is sent to any server.

Hover over any IQ badge and check the browser console (F12) for detailed calculations and breakdown.
