How It Works

The IQ score is calculated based on:

  • Word Length: Average length of words used
  • Vocabulary Diversity: Ratio of unique words
  • Sentence Complexity: Length and variance of sentences
  • Syllable Complexity: Average syllables per word
  • Readability Index: Text complexity score
  • Syntax Structure: Use of complex punctuation and clauses
